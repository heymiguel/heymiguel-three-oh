About Me_

who am I?

I'm a front-end (developer & designer). Every day I'm driven to merge the two, designing and programming experiences that are crisp, smooth, and clean. I seek to ensure that my client's experiences remain positive, productive, and empowering. The idea that I work in an industry that requires both creativity and tenacity is something that serves to inspire me daily.

When I'm not building things for the web, you can find me face deep in some science fiction, having a cup of coffee/ earl grey, or thinking up my next short story.
